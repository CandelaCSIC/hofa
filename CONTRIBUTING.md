    <h1>
          Contributing to the hofa project
    </h1>
</div>

Thank you for your interest in contributing to the **HoFa** package! We truly appreciate your enthusiasm and hope that this package will grow into a valuable resource for the community.

At this early stage of development, direct contributions to the repository are not yet possible. This is because we first need to obtain the necessary permissions from our institutions and establish a sustainable process for managing external contributions.

However, we would love to hear from you! If you’d like to:

- Report a bug,

- Suggest improvements, or

- Discuss potential contributions,

Please contact any of the authors directly. You can find their email addresses in their websites: 

- Pablo Candela: [Website Pablo](https://pablocandela.es/).

- Diego González-Sánchez: [Website Diego](https://dglez91.github.io/).

- Balázs Szegedy: [Website Balázs](https://users.renyi.hu/~szegedyb/).

We hope to open the package to community contributions in the near future, as we work toward building a collaborative environment for this exciting area of research. Stay tuned!
